My Story

The Path to Channeling

A Shamanic Beginning

I grew up in a shamanic household, deeply connected to spirit and energy work. Yet, tarot was considered taboo in my family. The cards called to me anyway, whispering from the edges of my awareness.

Montessori Teacher

I became a Montessori teacher, drawn to guiding young souls to discover their own inner wisdom. Teaching taught me to hold space, to trust intuition, and to see the divine in each individual journey.

College & The First Deck

In college, I bought my first tarot deck purely for creative exploration. I approached it as art, as storytelling. But something unexpected happened—the readings were accurate. Scarily accurate. People resonated deeply with the messages that flowed through.

Life Path 11 Awakening

I discovered I am a Life Path 11—the master number of intuition, illumination, and spiritual insight. It explained everything: my mediumship gifts, my connection to angel realms, and why channeling feels like remembering rather than learning. I now understand my purpose is to be a bridge between worlds.
